Interview with Hungry Bitches

We had the pleasure of interviewing Hungry Bitches, a fierce punk rock band from Los Angeles known for their high-energy performances and unapologetic lyrics. The band consists of lead vocalist and guitarist Mia, bassist Alex, and drummer Jordan. Their latest album, Rebel Yell, has been making waves in the punk scene.

Here's what they had to say:

GRIN: How did Hungry Bitches come together as a band?
Mia: We all met through the local music scene in LA. I was playing solo shows, and Alex and Jordan were in different bands. We clicked over our shared love for punk rock and decided to start something new together.

GRIN: What inspires your songwriting?
Mia: A lot of our songs are inspired by personal experiences and social issues. We want to use our music to speak out against injustice and empower our listeners.

GRIN: Your live shows are known for their energy. How do you prepare for a performance?
Jordan: We rehearse a lot to make sure we're tight as a band. But we also try to keep it spontaneous on stage – feeding off the crowd's energy really helps us bring that extra intensity.
