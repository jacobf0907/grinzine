Interview with Rosacade

Chicago’s screamo scene is on fire right now, and few bands embody that energy like Rosacade. Packing the house at Heavy Extreme Midwest Fest, the six-piece showed why they’re quickly becoming a cornerstone of the Midwest underground. We caught up with them after the set to talk origins, influences, the growing scene, and more. (Featured in Issue 2)

Interview with Weeping

Fresh off a night of unrelenting chaos at Beat Kitchen, we chatted with New Jersey’s own depressive homicidal death metal unit, Weeping. With last year’s Spiritual Barbarism still ripping skulls open with its violent riffage and suffocating intensity, the band—Theo, Bird, Tom, and Jon—continue carving their own twisted lane in extreme music. We talked origins, vocals, influences, and Malört in this one. (Featured in Issue 1)
